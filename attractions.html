Famous Attractions in Pampanga

Puning Hot Spring

The hot spring is located on Porac, Pampanga. It has 11 natural hot springs. The hot springs were created by the eruption of Mount Pinatubo in 1991. It also has a sand spa

Mount Arayat

It's located in Arayat and Magalang, Pampanga. It is a popular destination for hikers and it is also famous for its White Rock Formation.

Zoocobia Fun Zoo

It is located in Mabalacat City in Pampanga. They have ziplines, horseback riding, garden maze, labyrinth, and more!

Aqua Planet

Aqua Planet is a water park located in Clark, Mabalacat City, Pampanga. Aqua Planet offers different water slides like Aqua loop and Tornado, it also have wave pools, orc lagoon, and water river.

Dinosaurs Island

It is located in Mabalacat, Pampanga. They have giant animatronics and 3D dinosaur movies. They also have a Fossil Museum and a World od Fun.

Holy Rosary Parish Church

It is located in Angeles, Pampanga. It has unique architectural stlyes that reflect its historical development. The exterior of the church has a gothic appearance.
