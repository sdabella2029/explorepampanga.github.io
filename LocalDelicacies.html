Local Delicacies in Pampanga

Discover the flavorful local foods that symbolizes the culture of Kapampangans.

Local Delicacies in Pampanga

Sisig

It is made of pig's ears with garlic and chili peppers.

Batute

It is also called "Tugak". THe main ingeredient of this is a frog from rice paddies.

Kamaru

Kamaru is a delicacy made of mole crickets, they are also found in rice paddies.

Patko

It is a sweet dessert that is stuffed with fresh shredded coconuts and wrapped in a banana leaf.

Buro

It is a fermented rice mixed with fish or shrimp.

Turrones de Casoy

It is a nougat made with honey, sugar, egg white, and cashews.
