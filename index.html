About Pampanga

Rich History

Pampanga has a vibrant history dating back to pre-colonial times. It was one of the first provinces to be established by the Spaniards in the Philippines.

Culinary Capital

Known as the culinary capital of the Philippines, Pampanga offers a wide array of delicious dishes that reflect its rich cultural heritage.

Vibrant Festivals

Pampanga is famous for its colorful festivals, including the Giant Lantern Festival which attracts visitors from all over the world.
